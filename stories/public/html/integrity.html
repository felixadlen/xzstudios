We care about your privacy!

Here we have summorized how we at XZ gathers and stores your personal data. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact us.

Cookies and Personal data

We use cookies to be able to save your earlier choices and preferences, so you don't have to do it each time you visit our site. We also use cookies for marketing purposes. To measure the traffic on our site and optimize it so you as a visitor can have the best possible experience, we use Google Analytics. This is possible through third-party-cookies, which means that your data is being shared with Google, who can use it for customized advertisements. Here you can read more about Google's usage of cookies.

Storage & Management

We manage and stores your personal data in accordance with swedish law. It's only us at XZ who can access our statistics and we will store your personal data for as long as we find it necessary.

If you don't want to store cookies

You can always choose to turn off or clear your cookies in your web browsers settings. However, note that some services might not work the way they used to after you done this, since yout earlier choices, settings and history will be lost.
